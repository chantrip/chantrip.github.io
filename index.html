About

I am an Assistant Professor in the Computer Science / Data Science & AI programs at the Asian Institute of Technology (AIT), Thailand. My work combines AI, data-intensive systems, and inclusive technologies to support equitable access to information and services, especially for deaf and hard-of-hearing communities in ASEAN.

I collaborate with partners across Thailand, Malaysia, Indonesia, Japan, and beyond on building sign language corpora, translation systems, and AI-powered tools that can be deployed in real-world settings such as education, broadcasting, and public services.

Current interests
  • Large-scale sign language datasets and annotation workflows
  • Neural architectures for sign recognition and translation
  • LLM-augmented systems for accessible knowledge retrieval
  • Data engineering, MLOps, and trustworthy AI in practice
  • AI in education and technology for social good
